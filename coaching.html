My Methodology

The Self-Leadership Transformation Framework

A proven three-step process to get you unstuck, clarify your direction, and install a new business identity.

01

Observe

Clarify where you are now and where you want to be — your existing and desired business identity and goals.

02

Assess

Map your internal operating system. Discover the core beliefs, assumptions, and obstacles standing in your way.

03

Address

Install a new business identity by reprogramming your subconscious narrative through mindfulness meditation, cognitive reframing, and visualization.

Meet Your Coach

Douglas Uhlig, Ph.D., MBA

A uniquely multifaceted coach with global experience

Business & Life Coach

Dr. Doug Uhlig coaches professionals through workplace challenges, job insecurity, burnout, and stress. His rare combination of a Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology and an MBA in Marketing & Finance from NYU gives him unparalleled insight into both the business and human dimensions of leadership.

International Experience

Dr. Uhlig brings invaluable international perspective from his years living and working in Tokyo, where he held positions at Fuji Bank and the Industrial Bank of Japan. He is proficient in both Japanese and Chinese, enabling him to coach leaders navigating global business environments.

Wall Street & Corporate Leadership

With extensive experience in Human Resources at JP Morgan Chase, Citibank, and Bank of New York, Dr. Uhlig has provided leadership talent for major corporations. He has also worked with hedge funds and financial organizations in high-stakes environments.

Military Leadership

A retired Lt. Colonel in the Air Force, Dr. Uhlig served for 29 years. He provided team building and leadership training to over thirty Air Force reservists each year, cultivating discipline, strategy, and mission-focused leadership.
