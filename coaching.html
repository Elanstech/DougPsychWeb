Is This Right for You?

Coaching or Therapy?

They're different tools for different moments. Here's a simple way to tell which one fits where you are right now.

Therapy may be the better fit if you're…

  • Healing from trauma or a painful past
  • Struggling with anxiety or depression
  • Working through deeper emotional challenges
  • Looking to feel like yourself again
You're here

Coaching is the right fit if you're…

  • Functioning well, but ready for more
  • Growing your career or your business
  • Stepping up as a stronger leader
  • Facing a major life or career decision
  • Wanting a partner to keep you accountable

As both a licensed psychologist and a credentialed coach, I can help you pinpoint which is right for where you are — and we'll only move forward with coaching once it's clearly the best call for you.

How We'll Work

The Self-Leadership Transformation Framework

A proven three-step process that gets you unstuck, clarifies your direction, and builds the mindset and habits to perform at your best.

01

Observe

We get clear on where you are and where you want to be — your goals, your strengths, and what's really standing in the way.

02

Assess

We map your internal operating system and surface the core beliefs, assumptions, and blind spots holding you back.

03

Address

We replace the habits, self-doubt, and old stories in your way — with mindfulness, cognitive reframing, and visualization — so you lead with confidence, focus, and follow-through.

Meet Your Coach

Douglas Uhlig, Ph.D., MBA

A rare mix of Wall Street, boardroom, and psychology — in one coach

I bring together two worlds that rarely meet: high-stakes business and human psychology. With a Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology and an MBA from NYU, plus decades in banking, leadership, and the military, I help you understand not just what to change, but why the old patterns took hold — and how to replace them for good.

Business & Life Coach

I coach professionals through workplace challenges, job insecurity, burnout, and stress. A rare combination of a Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology and an MBA in Marketing & Finance from NYU gives me an insider's read on both the business and the human side of leadership.

International Experience

I bring hard-won global perspective from my years living and working in Tokyo, where I held positions at Fuji Bank and the Industrial Bank of Japan. Proficient in Japanese and Chinese, I coach leaders navigating cross-border business with confidence.

Wall Street & Corporate Leadership

Military Leadership
