Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

What should I expect at my first appointment?

Your initial consultation is primarily a conversation to understand your needs and establish comfort and trust. We'll discuss your reasons for seeking therapy, your background, and start developing a treatment plan tailored to your goals.

How long are therapy sessions?

Standard therapy sessions are 50 minutes. However, initial consultations may be longer to allow time for a thorough assessment of your needs.

How often will I need to attend therapy?

Most clients begin with weekly sessions. As progress is made, sessions might be scheduled less frequently. The frequency of therapy is tailored to your specific situation and goals.

Is everything I say confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of therapy. There are specific legal limitations to confidentiality that will be discussed during your first session, such as risk of harm to self or others.

Do you offer teletherapy/virtual sessions?

Yes, we offer secure video sessions for clients who prefer or require remote therapy. Our virtual platform is HIPAA-compliant to ensure your privacy.

How do I know if therapy is working?

Progress in therapy is regularly discussed and assessed. We'll establish clear goals and check in on your progress throughout treatment. Most clients report feeling better understood, having improved coping skills, and experiencing positive changes in their lives.
