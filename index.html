About Dr. Doug

Meet Your Expert Care Provider

A unique blend of traditional therapy and mindfulness practices

Dr. Doug Uhlig is a New York State licensed psychologist and psychotherapist who combines traditional Cognitive Behavioral Therapy with over 35 years of teaching Transcendental Meditation and Mindfulness.

Professional Certifications

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy - National Association for CBT
  • Gestalt Therapy – Three year institute training
  • Mindfulness Meditation - University of Washington
  • Certified Teacher of Transcendental Meditation
  • Certified Hypnotherapist - National Guild of Hypnotists
