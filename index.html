About Us

"Empowering individuals to overcome challenges and achieve lasting positive change through evidence-based therapeutic approaches."

Leadership & Vision

Under Dr. Doug Uhlig's leadership, our practice has evolved into a beacon of psychological excellence, combining traditional therapeutic wisdom with innovative approaches to mental health care. His dedication to comprehensive, accessible mental healthcare has transformed countless lives over three decades.

Our Philosophy

We believe in treating the whole person, not just symptoms. Our approach integrates the latest psychological research with deep empathy and understanding, creating a supportive environment where healing and growth flourish naturally.

Our Commitment

At Doug Uhlig Psychological Services, we're committed to providing exceptional care that's both professional and personal. Our team of experienced practitioners ensures every client receives the attention and support they need to thrive.
