Meet the founder and director of our practice

Dr. Douglas Uhlig, Ph.D.

Founder & Clinical Director

Ph.D., Clinical Psychology, Walden University
Licensed Clinical Psychologist
Board Certified, American Board of Professional Psychology

With over two decades of experience, Dr. Uhlig combines clinical expertise with a compassionate approach to help individuals, couples, and families achieve their goals.
