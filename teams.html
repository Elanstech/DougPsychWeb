Dr. Doug Uhlig

Clinical Psychologist, Founder
Leadership & Clinical Excellence

About

Dr. Doug Uhlig has over 20 years of experience in clinical psychology, specializing in cognitive behavioral therapy and evidence-based practices. As the founder of Doug Uhlig Psychological Services PLLC, he has built a practice dedicated to excellence in mental health care and personal growth.

His approach combines traditional therapeutic wisdom with innovative methodologies, ensuring each client receives personalized, compassionate care tailored to their unique needs and circumstances.

Angela Christina Ferri

Licensed Mental Health Counselor
Youth & Young Adult Therapy

About

As a licensed mental health counselor (LMHC), I specialize in supporting young adults who are grappling with issues of anger, low self-esteem and the anxiety associated with school performance. My goal is to empower individuals to take proactive steps in caring for themselves, fostering a sense of motivation and helping them become the best versions of themselves.

Additionally, I am experienced in working with clients experiencing depression, especially those who struggle with finding the drive to engage in daily activities. My approach is compassionate and client-centered, aiming to create a safe and supportive environment where each individual can explore their challenges and work towards meaningful change.

Areas of Expertise

  • Young Adult Mental Health
  • Anger Management & Emotional Regulation
  • Academic Performance Anxiety
  • Self-Esteem & Confidence Building
  • Depression & Motivation Enhancement
  • LGBTQ+ Affirmative Therapy

Therapeutic Approaches

Jaqueline Galynsky

Licensed Mental Health Counselor
Bilingual Therapy (English/Russian)

About

The therapeutic experience starts with you, and congratulations on taking the first step towards finding help for what has been weighing on you. Whether you're feeling overwhelmed by day-to-day stress, feelings of anxiety, or depression, my approach is to work collaboratively with you to create a safe and open therapeutic relationship.

Your needs as an individual are of my greatest concern, and my belief is that providing you with a non-judgmental, empathic, and welcoming space can promote the development of a healthier and happier lifestyle. Using cognitive behavioral therapy, rational emotive behavioral therapy, and mindfulness techniques, my goal is to help you address what's happening within.
