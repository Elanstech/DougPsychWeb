Complex Trauma

Our Process

Your Treatment Journey

A structured, evidence-based approach to psychological care

01

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough assessment to understand your unique needs, history, and goals. This comprehensive evaluation allows us to develop an effective treatment strategy.

02

Treatment Planning

Together, we'll develop a personalized treatment plan that addresses your specific concerns. This collaborative approach ensures your treatment aligns with your values and preferences.

03

Active Treatment

Using evidence-based therapeutic techniques, we'll work together to develop new skills, insights, and perspectives. Your treatment is adjusted based on your progress and evolving needs.

04

Progress & Maintenance

We'll regularly assess your progress and make adjustments as needed. As you achieve your goals, we'll develop strategies to maintain your progress and prevent relapse.
