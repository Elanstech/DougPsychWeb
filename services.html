Comprehensive Treatment

Depression & Mood Disorders

Depression and mood disorders can affect every aspect of your life, from relationships to work performance. Our evidence-based approach combines cognitive-behavioral therapy, interpersonal therapy, and medication management when appropriate to help you regain control and find joy in life again.

80% of depression cases respond positively to treatment
35+ years of clinical experience

Major Depressive Disorder

Our approach combines cognitive restructuring, behavioral activation, and lifestyle modifications to address persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest. Treatment includes identifying triggers, building coping skills, and developing healthy thought patterns.

  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy
  • Behavioral Activation
  • Mindfulness Techniques
  • Medication Management Support

Bipolar Disorder

Our comprehensive bipolar treatment focuses on mood stabilization, episode prevention, and functional recovery. We help you recognize early warning signs, develop routine management strategies, and build interpersonal effectiveness for improved relationships.

  • Interpersonal & Social Rhythm Therapy
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Techniques
  • Family-Focused Therapy
  • Relapse Prevention Planning

Persistent Depressive Disorder

For chronic depression, we utilize an integrative approach emphasizing long-term resilience building, narrative restructuring, and sustainable lifestyle changes. Our goal is to help you develop ongoing management tools for lasting symptom relief.

  • Cognitive-Behavioral Analysis System
  • Narrative Therapy Techniques
  • Acceptance & Commitment Therapy
  • Social Support Enhancement

Proven Techniques

Anxiety Treatment

Anxiety can feel overwhelming, but with the right strategies, you can develop mastery over your symptoms and reclaim your peace of mind. Our expert anxiety treatment combines cognitive-behavioral therapy, exposure techniques, mindfulness practices, and relaxation training to create a comprehensive approach tailored to your specific needs.

90% of clients report significant symptom reduction
6-12 sessions for substantial improvement

Generalized Anxiety Disorder

Our approach helps you break the cycle of persistent worry through cognitive restructuring, uncertainty tolerance training, and practical worry management techniques. We'll help you identify anxiety triggers and develop sustainable coping skills for long-term relief.

  • Cognitive Restructuring
  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction
  • Relaxation Training
  • Worry Time Management

Panic Disorder

Our panic disorder treatment includes panic cycle education, interoceptive exposure techniques, and cognitive restructuring to reduce fear of physical sensations. We develop personalized skills to manage panic symptoms and prevent attacks.

  • Panic Cycle Interruption
  • Interoceptive Exposure
  • Applied Relaxation Techniques
  • Situational Exposure Practice

Social Anxiety

Our specialized social anxiety program utilizes graduated exposure therapy, social skills training, and cognitive restructuring to help you overcome fears of judgment and rejection while building confident social interactions.

  • Graduated Exposure Therapy
  • Attentional Focus Training
  • Social Skills Development
  • Performance Confidence Building

Trauma-Informed Care

PTSD & Trauma Recovery

Trauma can fundamentally alter how you experience the world, but healing is possible. Our trauma-informed approach combines evidence-based protocols with compassionate care to help you process traumatic experiences, reduce distressing symptoms, and rebuild a sense of safety and control in your life.

85% reduction in trauma symptoms with specialized treatment
100% confidential, safe environment

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder

Our PTSD treatment utilizes evidence-based protocols including EMDR, Prolonged Exposure, and Cognitive Processing Therapy to address intrusive memories, avoidance behaviors, negative thoughts, and hyperarousal symptoms while helping you reclaim your sense of safety.

  • Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing (EMDR)
  • Cognitive Processing Therapy
  • Prolonged Exposure Therapy
  • Trauma-Focused CBT

Complex Trauma

For those who've experienced multiple or prolonged trauma, our approach focuses on establishing safety, processing traumatic memories, developing self-regulation skills, and rebuilding a cohesive sense of identity and meaningful connections.

  • Phase-Based Trauma Treatment
  • Internal Family Systems Therapy
  • Sensorimotor Psychotherapy
  • Schema Therapy

Acute Stress Disorder

Our early intervention approach for recent trauma utilizes targeted cognitive-behavioral techniques, self-regulation strategies, and supportive therapy to reduce acute stress symptoms and prevent the development of chronic PTSD.

  • Brief Cognitive-Behavioral Intervention
  • Acute Stress Management
  • Anxiety Reduction Techniques
  • Sleep Regulation Support

Our Process

Your Treatment Journey

A structured, evidence-based approach to psychological care

01

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough assessment to understand your unique needs, history, and goals. This comprehensive evaluation allows us to develop an effective treatment strategy tailored specifically to your situation.

02

Treatment Planning

Together, we'll develop a personalized treatment plan that addresses your specific concerns. This collaborative approach ensures your treatment aligns with your values and preferences while utilizing evidence-based approaches.

03

Active Treatment

Using evidence-based therapeutic techniques, we'll work together to develop new skills, insights, and perspectives. Your treatment is adjusted based on your progress and evolving needs to ensure optimal results.

04

Progress & Maintenance

We'll regularly assess your progress and make adjustments as needed. As you achieve your goals, we'll develop strategies to maintain your progress and prevent relapse, ensuring long-term well-being.
